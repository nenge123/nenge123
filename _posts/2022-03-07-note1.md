## RAR批量压缩

```bat
@echo off
::指定WinRAR安装路径和程序
set "rarpath=C:\Program Files\WinRAR\Rar.exe"
for /f "delims=" %%a in ('dir /b ^|find /v "%~nx0"') do "%rarpath%" a "%%~na" "%%a"
pause
```
